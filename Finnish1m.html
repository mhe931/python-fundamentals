Finland often experiences a "fifth season" called ruska.

A word can only contain vowels from one set (Back or Front), never both. Endings must match the vowels in the root.

  • Neutral Vowels (Neutraali): E, I (E, I) - Can mix with both, but the Back/Front vowels decide the suffix.
