Python Operators

Using operators in Python.

Operators

Operators are used to perform operations on variables and values. Python divides the operators in the following groups:

  • Arithmetic operators
  • Assignment operators
  • Comparison operators
  • Logical operators
  • Identity operators
  • Membership operators
  • Bitwise operators
Speaker notes: We will go through the most common ones.

Arithmetic Operators

Arithmetic operators are used with numeric values to perform common mathematical operations:


print(10 + 5)
print(10 - 5)
print(10 * 5)
print(10 / 5)
print(10 % 5)
print(10 ** 5)
print(10 // 5)

Logical Operators

Logical operators are used to combine conditional statements:


print(10 > 9 and 10 > 8)
print(10 > 9 or 10 < 8)
print(not(10 > 9 and 10 > 8))
